Biography

Simon Cerezo is an interactive/game design major of Filipino descent, from San Jose, California. He is an aspiring game designer, mainly focusing on map/level design. Growing up, he has always been a fan of video games, particularly games that allow the players to explore the environments around them. As a little boy, one of Simonâ€™s earliest memories would be that of his siblings playing video games on the two consoles we owned at the time, the Sega Dreamcast and the Sony Playstation 1. The games that his siblings would play contained, what was considered during their time, large-scale maps. While watching them play these games, it sparked something in a young Simon. It sparked his interest in games as well as his eagerness for personal customization, in hopes that someday he might possibly develop something similar in scale in the future.
