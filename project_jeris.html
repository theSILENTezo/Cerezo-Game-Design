Project Jeris

Overview

Project Jeris is a single-player walking simulator whose primary objective is to immerse the player in a nature-infused sci-fi environment and to let them explore an abandoned facility they have stumbled upon. The overall environment will not have any major gameplay elements, but will instead encourage players to walk around and explore what the map has to offer via its visuals.

Process

For the initial process of getting Project Jeris started, I began to sketch an environment that would be the blueprint of how the map would be layed out.

After the initial sketch, I went into the Unreal Engine and strated to layout the basic foundations for the environment. I started with a basic sky box, then added a terrain area, which I later sculpted to give it a more ridged feeling. Once the nature environment had been sculpted, I then moved on to indor environment and added more platforms.

Upon constructing the basic structures of the map, I then added textures, assets, and plug-ins. Once the textures and other assets had been added, I then moved onto inserting sound into the environment to help make the map more immersive.

The final step was then to set the time of day, that would best suit the environment and give the players/viewers a feel of curiosity.
